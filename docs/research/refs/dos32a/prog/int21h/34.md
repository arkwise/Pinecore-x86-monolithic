# 3.09 - DOS function 34h - Get InDOS Flag Address
| **In:** | **AH** = 34h |
|---|---|
| **Out:** | **ES:EBX** = address of InDOS flag |

**Notes: **

a) The selector returned in ES register will be zero selector, ie readable/writeable data selector with Base=0. This is an internal to DOS/32 Advanced selector which may NOT be freed or modified under any circumstances.
